Welcome to George Vineyard

George Vineyard is located in the Sonoma Valley viticultural area between Kenwood and Santa Rosa on the east side of Sonoma Highway. George Vineyard was initially planted in 1998 and 1999 on a south facing mountainside knoll bordering the Santa Rosa Creek. e unique parent soil composition is Felta gravelly loam in an abundance of stone. Many years have been spent clearing rock from the vineyard in order to grow cabernet sauvignon, syrah, petite syrah and riesling. Growing atop a mountain knoll creates an abundance of complexity in our grapes showcasing minerality in balance with rich mountainside fruit.
